Overview

Virtual Screen Manager is a Windows desktop application published by Catio Productions through the Microsoft Store. Your privacy matters to us. This app is designed to function entirely on your local device. We do not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information.

Data We Do Not Collect

Virtual Screen Manager does not collect, access, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, address, phone number)
  • Device identifiers or hardware information
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Files, documents, or content on your device
  • Browsing history or activity outside the app
  • Payment or financial information

Local Storage

Virtual Screen Manager saves your preferences locally on your device at %AppData%\Virtual Screen Manager\settings.json. This includes settings such as your chosen layout, theme, and panel position. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Window Management

To provide its core functionality, Virtual Screen Manager reads the titles and positions of open application windows on your screen. This information is used exclusively in memory to manage workspace switching and is never recorded, stored to disk, or transmitted anywhere.

Third-Party Services

Virtual Screen Manager does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. Purchases and license verification are handled entirely by the Microsoft Store and are subject to Microsoft's privacy policy. We do not receive or store your payment details.

Children's Privacy

Virtual Screen Manager does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected at all, this app is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If our data practices change in the future, this page will be updated with a new effective date. We encourage you to review it periodically.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Reach us at catio.productions.llc@gmail.com. We respond to all inquiries personally.
